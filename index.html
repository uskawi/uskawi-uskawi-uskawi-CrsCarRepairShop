Welcome To C.R.S Cars

Welcome to CRS Garage, your premier car repair shop located in the heart of Dublin city center at 5 Dorset Lane, D1, just off Dorset Street. With over 17 years of experience in the automotive repair industry, CRS Garage has established itself as a trusted name in car maintenance and repair. Our team of highly skilled technicians utilizes the latest technology and cutting-edge diagnostic equipment to ensure your vehicle receives the highest quality service. From routine maintenance to complex repairs, we are committed to delivering exceptional results and getting you back on the road safely and efficiently. At CRS Garage, we pride ourselves on our expertise and dedication to customer satisfaction. Our comprehensive range of services includes engine diagnostics, brake repairs, transmission services, and more. Whether you drive a compact car or a luxury vehicle, you can trust us to handle all your automotive needs with precision and care. Experience the difference at CRS Garage, where quality service and advanced technology come together to keep your car running at its best. Visit us today and let our experienced team take care of your vehicle.
